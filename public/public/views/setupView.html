Lime Stream Setup

Open OBS and click Settiings. Refer to the screenshots below:

General

Configure General tab to the screenshot below

Stream

Select Type: Custom Streaming Server

URL: hls (for ios platforms) Enter: rtmp://www.limestream.tv/hls

dash (for web browser and android platforms) Enter: rtmp://www.limestream.tv/dash

Stream Key: Enter a stream key i.e (example)

Copy URL and Stream Key somewhere for use later

Output

Configure your Output tab to the screenshot

You can check out other configuration tab settings

Click Ok

Display Your Stream

Connect your camera device to your computer

Right click the Scene tab and add a scene

Right click the Source tab, add Video Capture Device and click Ok

Device: Select your device name and click Ok.

You should see your camera device display output

Click Start Streaming… You will know if it’s screaming once the button change to Stop Streaming

Login to your Lime Stream profile.

Click your Live Stream button, click create and enter required details

Turn on your stream status and your are done.

You should now be able to see your stream the lists of live streams
